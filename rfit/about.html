About RFiT

Are you someone who wants to get fitter but theres just no time? Well, we hear you!

RFiT aims to provide an easy and hassle free online solution to help people integrate healthy habits into their busy lives!

Why Join RFiT?

  • Log from our extensive food and exercise database, count calories and hit your daily targets!
  • Personalized fitness goals, and daily calorie budgets set according to your body needs.
  • Exercise calorie estimates are based on your body weight and other details.
  • Stay hydrated! Our water tracker will keep you reminded to drink a glass of water whenever you can.
  • Awareness is the key to change! RFiT helps you watch your daily habits so you're motivated to make changes.
  • Track your progress till you reach your target, by logging your weight from time to time into the weight tracker.
